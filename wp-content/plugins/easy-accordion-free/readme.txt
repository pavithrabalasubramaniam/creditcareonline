== Installation ==

Installing this plugin as regular WordPress plugin.

After install, you are ready to use accordion in your post, page, widget or anywhere you like. Accordion can be created in two ways. Lets begun

<strong>1. Accordion form shortcode</strong>
		
Go to page > Add New. On the editor, you will see a dropdown called "EAF Shortcodes", click here. Then click on first menu called "Accordion form shortcode". In your post editor, you will see a shortcode. <code>[efaccordion id="01"] [efitems title="Your Title here ..." text="Your Text here ..."] [efitems title="Your Title here ..." text="Your Text here ..."] [efitems title="Your Title here ..." text="Your Text here ..."][/efaccordion]</code>


<strong>efaccordion</strong> is a wrapper shortcode for accordion item. You have to use this shortcode before & after accordion items shortcodes.You must use different id if you are planning to use multiple accordion in same page.

<p><strong>efitems</strong> is a shortcode for each accordion item. This shortcode has two attribute. <strong>title</strong> attribute is for accordion title & <strong>text</strong> is for accordion text. You can add your text as your wish. You can use multiple items in your accordion. Just you have to increase eitems shortcode.</p>

<strong>2. Accordion form post of custom post</strong>

If you wan to insert accordion form post, Add some post in Accordion items > Add New. Then use this shortcode: <strong>[eaf_items id="02"]</strong>

Now save your page, then visit the newly created page & see something awesome. :)
